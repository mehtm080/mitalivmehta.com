I interned at Jujan Markfin, a garment export house in summer 2016. It is one of the leading fashion houses with clients such as Zara, Next, El Corte, Forever 21 etc., creating garments with embroidery and sequin and exploring many other design techniques.
I created a business profile for them by collating the information provided by them. I took all photographs of their garments and of a model wearing their samples. I created a layout for the profile that has been used on all the pages and the color palette changes from page to page.
