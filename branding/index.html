This is a type driven campaign for a hypothetical event- Indian Film Festival in New York City. The event circles around the theme and idea of Bollywood films. I have designed a poster series, wayfinding signage, invite and a website for the event.
In the poster series, each poster has an iconic scene from Bollywood films and consists of details about the event. The background has a pop of color with attractive patterns, to bring out the drama, aesthetics and glory of Indian cinema.
