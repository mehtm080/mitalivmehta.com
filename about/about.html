My name is Mitali Mehta and I am a BFA Communication Design major at Parsons School for Design in New York City. I was born and raised in New Delhi, India and have always been interested in art and design.
Design is something that I have always been passionate about and I have decided to make this hobby, my profession. My design aesthetic is clean with mechanical dynamics and intricate user interfaces with a pop of color. I try to make the user experience more enjoyable by adding surprise elements wherever possible.
