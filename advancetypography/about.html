ABOUT
Indian Film Festical is a platform where the audience gets to watch some of the best Bollywood films. Seven days of screening, special events, red carpets and panel discussions take place. People from different cultural backgrounds get to experience the world of Bollywood in New York City.

The focus is to work with artists and creative organizations from India and allow their works to be showcased in front of a wider audience.
