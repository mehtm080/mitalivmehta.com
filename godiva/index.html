In November 2016 I participated in the “Redesign the Godiva Experience” competition and worked with three others on this. My team was amongst one of the winning teams and we were awarded third place.
We were inspired by the age-old tradition of writing secret letters, messages and codes to communicate unique and special messages only to be understood by those who have the key to the code. The idea is to celebrate everyday luxuries in everyday moments. The Godiva “Notebox” delivers a personalized and unique gift telling your story, you can indulge in and elevate your everyday moments, and save and share memories with a reusable and sustainable gift box.
There will be a digital interface placed on tablets in stores. On this the customer can select a theme, then chose the truffles they want and decide on a code index. Once the customer has gifted the box to their loved one then he/she can gain access to the code by scanning the QR code from the Godiva website.
The packaging is designed in a way that each chocolate unfolds the message in order. The compartment strip is made from PLA, a biodegradable material and the outer box is made from tin that provides a long product life.
