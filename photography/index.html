This is a mapping project in which I researched about the Highline in New York City, spent a lot of time walking on it and then decided on the topic “South East Asian Women Taking Selfies on the Highline”. I photographed a lot of women on different sections of the highline and then combined it together in the form of a map. The idea behind this was to be very specific while selecting a theme for mapping out the Highline. A transition in the background from daytime to sunset and then the night sky is also visible.
